Welcome to

FurnitureBase

You know that situation? There is this unique piece of furniture in your shop but the picture on your website hardly reflects the quality

Revolutionize Your

Online Presence

The VIME workflow transforms your product into a digital 3D version and enables a new form of online shopping. Thanks to the resulting high-resolution content, your customer gets the impression of physically standing in your shop and being able to evaluate the structure, color and workmanship of your product. This way, one can virtually set the desired chair into the living room and instantly modify its different features, view pictures or enjoy videos of the product.

All in

One

We create individual digital product presentations that match your requirements. Of course, we do not leave you alone with all the technical aspects. Depending on your requirements, we provide everything you need in order to successfully market your products and stay ahead of the competition - from counseling to the technical implementation and linkage with your IT infrastructure. Not only your customer saves time, money and stress but also you as entrepreneur. Since we offer a comprehensive package of service and ensure a smooth implementation and transition, you are able to focus on your operational core business.

Cost

You carry a product in ten, hundred or thousand different versions and want to display each in your online shop without having to take a separate photo of every possible modification? In this case, we have good news for you: Your product can be captured directly on-site only once and your customer can select the full range of styles online with only one click. On top of this, the digital 3D animations can be easily integrated as pictures into your website and – in your desired environment – into your print catalog.
